Church Support

A lot of pastors are doing good work with very little. We want to change that — by sending construction teams, providing funding, and meeting practical needs that fall outside what a congregation can cover on its own. The goal is to get behind what God is already doing in local churches and give it room to grow.

Pro-Life Outreach

The cost of pregnancy is one of the real reasons women choose abortion. We want to take that off the table. By working with hospitals, churches, and pro-life organizations, we are building a way to fund pregnancies directly and walk alongside women who need more than a pamphlet. This is long-term work that requires long-term funding.
