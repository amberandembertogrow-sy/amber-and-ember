Our Initiatives

What we're working on

These are the three areas where Amber & Ember is doing real work. Each one is in development, but the need is real and the work has started.

🏗️  Church Support

A lot of pastors are doing good work with very little. We want to change that — by sending construction teams, providing funding, and meeting practical needs that fall outside what a congregation can cover on its own. The goal is to get behind what God is already doing in local churches and give it room to grow.

🤝  Pro-Life Outreach

The cost of pregnancy is one of the real reasons women choose abortion. We want to take that off the table. By working with hospitals, churches, and pro-life organizations, we're building a way to fund pregnancies directly and walk alongside women who need more than a pamphlet. This is long-term work that requires long-term funding.

📖  Discipleship

Young Christians need more than information. They need someone in their corner who can help them understand what the Bible says and how to apply it — to their marriage, their workplace, their everyday decisions. The discipleship initiative covers seminars, resources, and the content side of the ministry.
