Pro-Life Outreach

Standing for life by removing the barriers that lead women toward abortion.

The Need

Cost is one of the real reasons women choose abortion.

Pregnancy is expensive. For a woman without support â€” financially, relationally, or both â€” the cost of carrying a child to term can feel impossible. We believe that if you remove the financial pressure, a lot of women make a different choice. That is the premise this initiative is built on.

How We Work

Partnerships, not programs.

Hospital & Clinic Partnerships

Working directly with medical providers to cover pregnancy-related costs for women who cannot afford them on their own.

Church Networks

Connecting with local churches already doing pro-life work to fund and resource what they are building.

Community Outreach

Spreading awareness through like-minded networks so women who need support know it is available.
