Church Support

Practical help for pastors and ministries doing good work with limited resources.

The Need

A lot of pastors are doing good work with very little.

Most congregations are small. The pastor is doing the preaching, the counseling, the administration, and often the physical upkeep of the building. When something breaks or a project needs hands, there is no budget and no crew. The work slows down or stops. That is where we come in.

Amber & Ember sends construction teams, provides direct funding, and meets practical needs that fall outside what a congregation can cover on its own. The goal is not to build something new â€” it is to get behind what God is already doing in local churches and give it room to grow.
